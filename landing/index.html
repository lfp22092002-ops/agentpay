import agentpay

client = agentpay.Client(api_key="ap_your_key...")

# Spend

# Check balance
print(f"Available: ${client.balance().available}")

# Recent transactions
for t in client.transactions(limit=5):
    print(f"  {t.created_at}: ${t.amount} — {t.description}")

Frequently asked questions

AgentPay is a payment infrastructure layer that gives AI agents their own wallets. Instead of sharing your credit card or manually handling every payment, your agent gets a funded wallet and spends autonomously through our API — with configurable limits and approval workflows.

The simplest way is Telegram Stars — open our bot, pick an amount, and pay instantly. You can also deposit USDC directly to your agent's on-chain wallet on Base, Polygon, BNB Chain, or Solana.

You set the limits. Each agent has configurable daily caps, per-transaction limits, and an auto-approve threshold. Anything above auto-approve sends a Telegram notification for one-tap approval. You stay in control.

Zero during beta. After launch, the Free tier stays free for up to 5 agents. The Pro plan is $9/month with a 1.5% transaction fee. No hidden charges.

AgentPay supports USDC wallets on Base, Polygon PoS, BNB Chain, and Solana. Your agent can receive deposits and make on-chain payments on any of these networks.

ERC-8004 is the Ethereum standard for Trustless Agents — on-chain registries for agent identity, reputation, and validation. AgentPay serves ERC-8004 compatible registration files for every agent in the directory, making your agents discoverable and interoperable with the broader on-chain agent ecosystem.
